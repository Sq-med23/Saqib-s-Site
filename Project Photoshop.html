Below are photoshopped images that I have made:

First, the image that you are seeing is a dog that I took a picture of in Saudi Arabia. The other images, like the galaxy background, the moustache, and the chef’s hat, were taken from a website that provided copyright-free images. I opened Adobe Photoshop and created a project using an outer space background image. After that, I opened the photo of the dog in a separate file and masked it. Using the selection tool, I carefully outlined the dog and removed the background.

Once the dog was fully masked, I placed it onto the space background. Then I added the chef’s hat and the pink moustache to give the dog more personality. I adjusted everything so it fit properly. Finally, I added a speech bubble describing the dog as an Arabian chef. I used four images total and tried to blend everything smoothly.



Above is an eye that I created in Adobe Illustrator. The theme was an alien, droopy yolk eye with curly eyelashes and stars. It gives a weird but funny feeling.



In this image, I used a picture of 59th Street with buildings all around. I did not include every detail, but I focused on the main parts like the street and buildings. I used the pen tool to create shapes and outlines, and I improved over time.
